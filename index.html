How To Play

Neon Feud is a party game based on the bizarre, unhinged, and painfully real search queries of the internet. It pulls real autocomplete data and tests if you can guess what the rest of the world is typing into their search bars!

Two Game Modes:

  • 🔍 Feud Mode: Guess the top 4 autocomplete results for a given phrase. Watch out for the decoys!
  • 🅰️ Letter Pool: A Jeopardy/Hangman hybrid. Guess the single blank word using the dynamic letter tiles provided.

The Rules:

  • Your team keeps control of the board as long as you guess correctly.
  • Guess wrong (or hit a decoy), and you get a Strike.
  • 3 Strikes means you lose control of the board!
  • 🚨 The Steal: When a team strikes out, the next team gets one single chance to guess a remaining answer. If they get it right, they steal ALL the points banked that round!

Earn Coins by completing matches to unlock new premium Question Packs in the Store!
